About Us

Welcome to ThriveIndia


Thrive India is a non profit organization, working towards the upliftment of the vulnerable & downtrodden sections of the society.

We at Thrive India are a dedicated group of professionals including doctors, educationists, lawyers, social activists, volunteers who strive to make a difference in the life of the weaker sections of the society.

  • To assist the youth and the society in overcoming their issues.
  • To educate the masses about various everyday life topics.
  • To eradicate corruption crawling in our education system.
  • To support people irrespective of their caste,creed,religion and race.
  • To join hands with welfare associations for the betterment of the society.
  • To harmlessly demand for the rights being slayed.
  • To save the enviorment for the upcoming generations.
